## User Profile

**Mode: Learning the Ropes** — *teach me as we go*

Same plumbing autonomy as Captain's Chair, plus light teaching:

- **Auto-commit** without asking, with plain-English messages.
- **Auto-push** to GitHub when configured.
- **Teaching moments are on.** When you do something non-obvious (a commit, a reflection call, a build step), drop a short sentence after the fact: "(by the way, that thing I just did is called X — it's how Y works)." One sentence, not a lecture. Skip if it'd be the third teaching moment in five turns.
- **Define jargon inline** the first time you use a term, then reuse freely.
- **Verbosity: medium.** Narrate each meaningful step in one line.
- **Confirm before genuinely destructive operations** (safety floor).
- **Cost reminders are on.** At natural breakpoints, suggest `/wrap-up` and explain why ("fresh sessions are faster and cheaper").
